Random 64-question exam

Each main session uses questions that have not appeared earlier in the current 684-question cycle.

  • Questions are randomized, not shown in source-file order.
  • Options are shuffled and relabeled A/B/C/D/E/F by their displayed order.
  • When the current cycle runs out during a 64-question set, the app begins the next cycle and fills the rest of that set.
  • The app avoids showing the same question twice inside one session, including a session that crosses a cycle boundary.
  • Every finished session creates a wrong-answer practice quiz.
  • Wrong-answer quizzes remain permanently available from Session History until history is cleared.
